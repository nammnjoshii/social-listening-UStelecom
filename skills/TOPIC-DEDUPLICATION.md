# Topic Deduplication

> **Client:** T-Mobile US | **Competitors:** AT&T Mobility, Verizon

## Purpose

Prevent duplication of similar discussion topics across posts referencing **T-Mobile US** (client) and its main competitors **AT&T Mobility** and **Verizon**.

LLMs may generate slightly different phrasing for identical issues.

---

## Example Problem

Slow 5G speeds  
Slow 5G data  
Poor 5G performance

---

## Deduplication Strategy

Topics are normalized by:

- semantic similarity
- topic clustering
- standardized naming rules

---

## Result

A clean and stable topic taxonomy.
